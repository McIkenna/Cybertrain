    CYBERSECURITY LESSONS


    Welcome to lessons in Cyber Security. Here you find several lessons that can teach you safe habits while searching online and communicating with your friends and classmates. Please be aware at all times and know who it is your communicating with.



    Please always be aware, and if something doesnt look or fell right, notify an adult so that they may interact. No one should ever ask for your private information such as address, phone number, social security number, or any personal questions about you or your family. Remain aware just as Security Sam and enjoy your experience researching online and learning new and exciting things.
